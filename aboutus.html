Welcome!

Shivam Gupta - Founder

My name is Shivam Gupta. I was born and raised in Atarra, Uttar Pradesh. I have done my schooling from Tathagat Gyansthali Senior Secondary School Atarra (Banda) Uttar Pradesh.

At present, I am pursuing B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering from St. Aloysius' College (autonomous) Jabalpur M.P. and with this, I am running a Non-profit organization - Mission Needy People where we help needy people.

We see nowadays old people, children, and women sitting roadside and outskirts hungry with the spark of hope in their eyes that someone will come and help us.

We know that most of us want to help them but because of some or the other reason we are unable to help them. But by connecting with our Mission Needy People (MNP), you all can help these needy people.

You can provide them food, clothes, donation, and education via our MNP. It's my humble request to all those reading this message - please help us in this noble cause.

If everyone starts thinking about himself, then who will think about those people who are lying destitute on the road? If you think about the families of other needy people except your family, then it is absolutely right.

— Our Philosophy
