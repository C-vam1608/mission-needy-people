Jeene ke liye sirf kuch hi cheezo ki jarurat hoti hai - Pet bharne ke liye khana, Pine ke liye pani, Saas lene ke liye clean air aur body ko cover karne ke liye kapde.

You know one thing, is duniya mein do tarah ke log hote hain - ek jinke paas bahot paisa hota hai aur dusre jinke paas ek bhi paise nahi hota hai. Jinke paas paisa hota hai vo ek achha se achha khana khaate hain, kapde pahente hain, ghumte hain aur paise ka kai galat jagah pe use karte hain.

Road pe rehne walo ka koi nahi hota jo ki subah se apne pet ko bharne ke liye logo se paise maangte hai. Jinke bachhe log apne mata pita ko ghar se nikaal dete hain aur vo sadko pe aakar apna kisi tarah se pet bharte hain.

Kitni gandi situation hai hamari is duniya ki - jo mata pita bachpan se apne bachhe ko palte hain, unka pura khyal rakhte hain, vahi log bade hokar apne mata pita ko sadak pe le aate hain.

Aaj bhi insaniyat jinda hai aur log aaj bhi help karne ke liye aage badh rahe hain.

Aap sabhi daaniyon ki vajah se unko ek time ka khana naseeb ho jaata hai. Aap logo ka main bahot aabhar vyakt karna chahunga jo aisa kaam karte hain aur jo kar nahi paate lekin daan dekar vo un besahara logo ki help karte hain.

Aap sabhi log ke ek chote se daan se un gareebon ka pet bhar sakega. Aapki ek din ki chai ke paise se ek gareeb ka khana aa sakega agar aap log support karenge to. So please support me and my team.

Main har ek chote se choti jagah par jaakar logo ki help karta hu. Har jagah meri team hai jisse ki main jitne log ho sake unki help kar saku.
